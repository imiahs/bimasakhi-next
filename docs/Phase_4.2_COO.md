# 🔷 4.2 Chief Operating Officer (COO)

## Objective

Ensure smooth execution, delivery, and operational efficiency across all business processes.

## System Nature

* 60–80% Automation (Rules + Systems)
* 20–40% Human intervention

## Core Output

Execution → Delivery → Efficiency

## Core Dependencies

* CMO → Leads & demand
* CSO → Sales commitments
* CFO → Budget & resources
