# 🔷 4.4 Chief Sales Officer (CSO)

## Objective

Convert qualified leads into revenue and maximize customer lifetime value through structured, deterministic, and scalable sales systems.

## System Nature

* 60–70% Rule-based execution
* 20–30% Automation (CRM + workflows)
* 10–20% Human closing intelligence

## Core Output

Leads → Conversion → Revenue → Retention

## Core Dependencies

* CMO → Leads & intent data
* COO → Service delivery
* CFO → Pricing & payment structure
* CTO → CRM & automation systems

# 🔷 4.4.1 Lead Management / Inbound Sales Team

## Objective

Convert incoming leads into qualified opportunities

---

### 4.4.1.1 Lead Qualification Specialist

* Objective: qualify leads
* Input: lead data (source, intent)
* Output: qualified / rejected lead
* Decision Rules:

  * IF intent = high → assign to closer
  * IF incomplete → request info
* Tools: CRM
* AI: lead scoring
* Human: final validation
